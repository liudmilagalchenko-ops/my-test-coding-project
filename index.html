Questions & Answers

Find quick answers to the most common questions about plans, billing, and usage.

What is the right plan for me?

Hobby is great for exploring, Pro is best for individual daily use, and Business plans fit teams that need collaboration and admin controls.

What are my payment options?

We accept major credit and debit cards. Business customers can also request invoice-based billing on Enterprise.

How does usage-based pricing work?

Each plan includes generous baseline limits. If you exceed them, additional usage is billed at a transparent per-unit rate.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for teams?

Teams and Enterprise plans include volume-based discounts. Contact sales to tailor a package for your organization.

Is my data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, with additional security and compliance options on Enterprise.

Can I try Business plans before committing?

Our team can help you run a pilot with a subset of users so you can validate fit before rolling out broadly.
